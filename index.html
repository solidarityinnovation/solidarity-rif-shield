Know Your Rights
Retention StandingUnder 5 CFR Part 351, employees are ranked by tenure, veterans preference, and performance for RIF actions.
Veterans PreferenceVeterans receive additional retention points - 20 pts for disabled, 10 pts for others (5 CFR 351.501).
Bump and Retreat RightsYou may have rights to bump lower-standing employees or retreat to a former position (5 CFR 351.701).
Appeal RightsRIF actions can be appealed to MSPB within 30 days. Union representation available (5 CFR 351.901).
AFGE RepresentationAs an AFGE member, you have the right to union assistance in any RIF proceedings.
Severance PayEligible employees separated by RIF may receive severance pay under 5 CFR Part 550, Subpart G.
